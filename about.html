About

I am a computational linguist working on historical linguistics automation,computational approaches to comparative reconstruction, and diachronic simulations. My research combines data-driven models and domain knowledge from field linguistics, for historical linguistic inference.

Research interests

  • Computational historical inference and simulation
  • Protoform reconstruction and cognate detection
  • Language contact & loanword detection
  • Reproducible pipelines for linguistic data processing
