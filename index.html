Promise Dodzi Kpoglu holds a PhD in Linguistics from the University of Lille and Leiden University in the Netherlands. He specializes in African languages, particularly the Kwa languages. In recent years, his research has focused on computational linguistics, with a special interest in applying machine learning and deep learning models to the study of African languages.

Recruited within the BANG project for this purpose, he contributes his expertise in computer science to enhance our understanding of the phylogeny of Bangime. Thus, on the BANG project, he currently works on computational methods for historical and comparative linguistics, simulation of language trajectories, and coding reproducible pipelines for computational historical inference.
